## Description

Our online comic book store is a user-friendly platform designed for comic book enthusiasts. The app provides a convenient way to browse, search for, and purchase comic books. Below are some of the key features of our app:

## Search Functionality

- **Search by Title or Author:** Users can easily search for comic books by title or author name. This search feature makes it effortless to find specific comic books in our extensive catalog.

## Book Information

For each comic book, the application displays:

- **ID:** A unique identifier for the comic book.
- **Release Date:** The date the comic book was released.
- **Price in Cents:** The cost of the comic book in cents.
- **Name:** The title of the comic book.
- **Author:** The name of the author(s) of the comic book.

## Shopping Cart

Users have the option to create and manage their shopping cart. The cart allows users to:

- **Add Books:** Easily add comic books to the shopping cart.
- **Delete Items:** Remove items from the cart if they change their mind or no longer wish to purchase a specific comic book.
- **Switch Items:** Replace one book with another if they find a different title they prefer.
- **Clear Cart:** The ability to clear the entire cart in case they want to start fresh.

## Why Choose Us

- **Convenience:** Our platform provides a seamless shopping experience with a user-friendly interface.
- **Extensive Catalog:** We offer a vast selection of comic books, ensuring that users can find titles that suit their preferences.
- **Customization:** Users have control over their shopping cart, allowing them to manage their selections as they see fit.
